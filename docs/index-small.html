You are a Wizzard !

So now that you know, you have a BIG task: defeat the dark side!

Here are some tips that will help you achieve that goal:

Use the arrow keys or the arrow buttons to move the broom around

Press the WAND button to shoot spells at your enemies

Press the SPACE key to pause the game

Press the S key to shoot spells at your enemies

Friends and Enemies

Be Careful of Dementors! They work for the Dark Lord so stay away from them or you'll lose 200 points and will cost you a life

But don't worry Owls come for your aid! So you better catch up with them to gain back the lives you have lost or earn 250 points.

Don't forget to collect the Golden Snitches! Each will give you 150 points!

Final Boss

When you reach the final stage you will encounter one of these evil wizzards:

Shoot some spells until you defeat them and you'll be a champion!
